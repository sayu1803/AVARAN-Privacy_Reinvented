Create Encryption Password

This password will be used to encrypt and decrypt your files.

Note: If lost, this password cannot be recovered. You can change it, but the encrypted files will be lost. If you wish to skip this step, you won't be able to upload encrypted files.

Password Requirements:

At least 10 characters long
At least 1 uppercase letter
At least 1 lowercase letter
At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)
